Welcome to the Melbourne scavenger hunt

Objective

Solve the riddles and puzzles to reveal, explore, and eat your way through some of my favourite places in Melbourne

How to play

Swipe left or right to cycle through the questions.
Use Google Maps to help you find locations.
Use the pencil button at the bottom left of the screen to write your answers.

Map

Mark your progress on the map by drawing your route. Return to the map at any stage via the menu.

Swipe right to start. Good luck!
